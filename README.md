This application was built to digitize the operations of **Sanjay Itta Udhyog**, a brick manufacturing business. Previously, the business relied on manual bookkeeping, which led to:
*   Difficulty in tracking real-time inventory of different brick types (No.1, No.2, No.3).
*   Complex calculations for labor wages and payments.
*   Lack of instant insights into daily sales and expenses.

This **ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)** solution automates these processes, providing a centralized dashboard for business owners to manage their entire workflow efficiently.
